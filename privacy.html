Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. LookieLoo is designed with privacy in mind.

Data Collection

We don't collect anything.

LookieLoo operates entirely on your local Mac and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, usage analytics, or information about your activities.

Local Operation

All functionality of LookieLoo happens locally on your device:

  • Media controls work directly with your installed applications
  • File management stays within your local file system
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No analytics or tracking are performed

Permissions

LookieLoo may request certain macOS permissions to function properly:

  • AppleScript: Required for media controls with target media apps

These permissions are used solely for the app's functionality and no data is collected or transmitted.

Updates

This privacy policy may be updated to reflect changes in the app. Any updates will be posted on this page. The app itself does not collect data about when or how you use it.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please reach out through our Discord community.
